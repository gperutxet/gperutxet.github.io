§ 02 About

My practice spans the space between drawing and making — from the initial geometric logic of a form to its realisation in material through robotic and digital fabrication processes.

Working across steel, timber, composite, and concrete, I develop commissions and installations that are shaped as much by the constraints of manufacturing as by spatial intention. Each project begins with a question about how something can be made, not only what it should look like.

Alongside studio practice, I maintain an active research engagement with computation, material behaviour, and the expanded field of architectural fabrication.
